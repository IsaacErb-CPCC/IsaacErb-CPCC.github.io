Our Mission

Our mission at Lil'RoboCo (Lilith RoboticsCorp) is to create lovable, intelligent lifestyle-assistance robots, and make them available to fit into the lives of common consumers. Unlike the cleaning-bots of yester-generation, or the converations-AIs and companionship-droids of more recent times, what we seek to provide are robotic companions who offer support in both an emotional and functional sense.

For example, take our first-ever product: The AmobeaPet (original name: AmobeaDog/Cat/Hedgehog,Etc.); it is a high-quality floor-crawling cleaner-robot, with functionality on-par with any of those you might find without an absurd price-tag attached to them. However, because it's made by us, it also greets you as you pass by, enjoys being pet, and will get lonely if haven't paid attention to it in a while. And these are not primative, poorly-implemented features; they are driven by sophisticated AI developed by our computer-scientists over in R&D. And all of that, we offer to you at prices competitive with medium-high-end machines with the same cleaning-functionality.

¿How is that a sustainable business-model, especially if you're doing a bunch of R&D?, you might ask. Well, we're counting on you to give us enough business to let us keep making our dream a reality. ¿And don't you wanna?; C'mon: Robopets. Robofriends. Robospouses. ¡We can actually make those happen!

(That last part curtesy of the founder, Skog Irons.)

About Our Branding

As with the default setting of the digital displays on our robot-models, the general idea for the aesthetic of our website is futuristic sci-fi; we want users to feel that they're reading a holographic-terminal-display from a sci-fi movie. (We experimented with actual holographic-display technology, but found it quite hard to actually read.)

Colors

For our text-colors, we go with a base of bright-green on midnight-blue, for that digital feel. For highlights, we use amber and white, for contrast.

Text

For our font, we use Fira Sans, for its modern, slightly-futuristic look.
