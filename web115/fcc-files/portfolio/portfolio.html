My Projects:

Iron Cast (Font)

A semibold-weight, monospaced sans-serif font I made because I wanted a good general-purpose monospace. It is stylistically based off of Bahnschrift, which is stylistically based off of DIN 1451.

Carbon-13 (Font)

My own take on how the classic ‘dot-matrix’ style of font should look. This style of font is nothing new; if you've played Minecraft, you've seen it. But all-such fonts I've seen do things I don't like, so I decided to make my own.

Riika & Chineu (Book)

This is the second book I've written, and is basically an immigration-story, with protagonist's character-arc focusing on a conflict between curiosity and fear of the unknown.
